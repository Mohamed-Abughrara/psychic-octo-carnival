Services

Albian Shipping Agency offers services to the specific needs of our clients and we have the ability to meet the growing demands of our customers. Our services include:

  • Shipping Agency.
  • Freight of all kinds (sea and air).

  • Transport and unloading and packaging.
  • Export in accordance with the technical specifications adopted.
